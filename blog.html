My lab partner and I headed to the lab today to finish our midterm project--an amplifier circuit for a speaker! We succeeded... kind of. There is a slight bug in our circuit which isn't allowing our variable resistor to act as a volume knob. My partner and I decided that we should revisit this project when the quarter is over. I'm looking foward to going throught the schematic/components and finding the cause of this issue. We also plan on designing and 3D printing an enclosure! My tip: do all of your soldering in one sitting!
